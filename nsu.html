North South University

Summer 2017 - Fall 2021

I started this new phase of my life as a university student in the Summer of 2017. I suddenly discovered myself in a busy city, surrounded by unfamiliar people and locations. It was both an exciting and intimidating experience. I was eager to begin this new stage of my life, but I was also nervous because I was leaving everything I knew behind and stepping into the unknown.

Despite my initial anxiety, I adapted to my new life as a university student very quickly. I joined a club, and my friends and I formed a robotics team. We traveled to different universities to compete in robotics tournaments, so I was over the moon when our team won first runner-up in one of the competitions. Working as a team to accomplish something unique was an advantageous experience.

Along with my academic and extracurricular activities, I volunteered to help my university put together several programs. This allowed me to meet new people from various backgrounds and give back to my community. Working on research projects with my professors gave me additional beneficial experience. Exploring a subject I was passionate about and learning from subject-matter authorities was fascinating.

I earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering in the Fall of 2021. I was thrilled to begin the next phase of my life and proud of everything I had accomplished at university.
