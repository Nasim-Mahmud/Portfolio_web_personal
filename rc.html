Rajshahi College

June 2013 - April 2015

The excitement of transitioning from school to college was intense. I couldn't wait to explore alone and meet new people. After spending seven years in school with familiarity, I enjoyed the challenge of a new environment. Also, it was time to get ready for the challenging higher secondary exam. But that didn't stop me from making new friends, spending a ton of time in school, attending my tuition, and playing football.

One of the most prestigious universities in the nation, my college expected excellence from every student. The professors tested us to the limit, but their rigor eventually paid off. Our college topped the division in board results, and I was proud to be a part of that achievement. My two years in college went by quickly, and I graduated with a strong academic foundation and a lifetime of memories.
