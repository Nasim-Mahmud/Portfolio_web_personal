My resume

An overview of my educational journey

I think this is a journey that never ends, and everyone of us has our own interpretation of it. At the age of 5, I took the first step on this adventure in a primary school. I began preparing for the entrance exam for one of the prestigious government schools in Rajshahi while I was in second grade. And the school accepted me!

After I started secondary school, it was a roller coaster journey for me. I met a lot of new people, but as the days went on, everything started to feel more familiar. Made a lot of friends, got to know some caring teachers, and created a lot of memories. Participated in a few extracurricular activities and, for the first time in my life, went on a school trip with friends. Passed the board exams with flying colors.

It was a challenging but gratifying experience to attend college or higher secondary. For two years, I underwent rigorous preparation to perform well on the board exam. Everyone was busy, even myself. I played a lot of football, took many exams, spent many sleepless nights, and tirelessly studied. Did make it into a few government universities, but I dreamt of being an engineer. So, I was admitted to a private university after graduating. I successfully completed my bachelor's degree in computer science and engineering. I was involved in a student chapter, I founded a robotics team with a few of my friends and helped plan some well-attended events at my institution. Additionally, I did some research for two of my professor in computer science. I am currently working on a research paper with one of my professor.

For me, it was both a fulfilling and challenging journey. I'm hoping that this adventure will lead me to new areas and educate me throughout time because I still have a lot to learn.
